about me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the department of Statistics and Probability at Michigan State University. For my research, I am utilizing word embedding algorithms with generalized additive models to develop new methods for extracting and analyzing paragraphs of text in the context of insurance risk. Outside of school, my favorite hobby is learning about Statistics through sports. As I hear about a new technique, I dig deeper by applying the method to answer a variety of sports related questions.
